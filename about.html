Work Ethics

We have some set of work ethichs we strictly follow to maintain the good relationships between the customers and company. Please read the rules and regulations carefully that helps us to move the business smoothly.

  • The remaining fee must be paid after the work is completed

  • The advance will not be refunded, 4 days after the commencement of works
  • Notice must be given within one month of the commencement of work if any changes are to be made in the work or plan
  • Refunds will be given if customers wish to drop the plan
  • Customers can change the requirements according to the plan of their choice
  • Customers can hold any number of business meetings based on their plan
